What is AI Doctors Portal?

How does it work?

AI Doctors Portal works by allowing doctors to record patient conversations on their mobile devices or laptops. It converts the spoken conversation into text in real-time, making documentation efficient and accurate.

Recording Instructions

Template Selection:

Create a custom template or choose the default template.

New Consultation Page:

Enter the Patient's Name.

Start Consultation:

Click on the "Start Consultation" button to initiate recording of the doctor-patient conversation.

Finish and Generate Notes:

After recording the interaction, select the "Finish and Generate Notes" button.

Processing Notes:

Processing the clinical notes will take between 30 seconds to 2 minutes.

Once the output is generated, you can modify it using the microphone to input notes orally or by copying notes.

Download Options:

Download the output audio and notes in PDF/Word format for future reference.

Data Management:

Data will be deleted after 72 hours for security purposes. Ensure to download data for Electronic Medical Record (EMR) and direct copy-paste for EMR purposes.

***If there are mistakes in what you say in real-time transcription, AI will fix them when you finish the consultation. The written version is only there to check if your microphone and computer are working right.
